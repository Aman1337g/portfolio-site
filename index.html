Intro

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Aman Kumar Gupta, and I am a full-stack web developer currently studying at IIIT Bhubaneswar. I have a keen interest in problem-solving and enjoy developing innovative solutions to complex challenges.

My passion for coding started with C and C++ programming languages during my school days, and I continued to explore my interest in programming by learning Python. As a full-stack web developer, I have experience with both front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and MongoDB.

I have worked on several web development projects, including e-commerce platforms, social media applications, and online learning management systems. I am dedicated to delivering high-quality, user-friendly web solutions that meet my clients' needs.

In my free time, I enjoy participating in coding competitions and exploring new technologies. I believe that continuous learning and self-improvement are essential for staying up-to-date in the ever-evolving field of web development.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work!. By the way, check out my awesome work.
